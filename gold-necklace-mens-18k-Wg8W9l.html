Understanding Magnetic Force: Why Metal Poses a Critical Risk: men's chain materials

MRI machines utilize incredibly powerful magnetic fields, thousands of times stronger than the Earth's natural magnetic field. Ferromagnetic metals (like iron, steel, and often components in gold alloys) react strongly to these fields, becoming rapidly attracted to the magnet. Even non-ferromagnetic metals (like pure gold or silver) can heat up due to induced electrical currents. This phenomenon, known as "projectile effect" or "missile effect," explains why any metallic necklace for men chain poses a critical risk.

The rapid acceleration of a men's chain towards the machine can cause severe injury to the patient or staff. Regardless of the specific men's chain materials, if it's metallic, it's a risk.
