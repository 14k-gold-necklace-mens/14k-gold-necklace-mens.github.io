Understanding Gold Purity: What is 14k gold in jewelry?

Gold purity is measured in karats (k), with 24 karats representing 100% pure gold. However, pure gold is exceptionally soft and not suitable for crafting durable jewelry. This is where alloys come into play, enhancing the gold's strength and color.

14k gold contains 58.3% pure gold, alloyed with other metals like copper, silver, or zinc. This specific composition provides a robust and resilient metal, perfect for active lifestyles.
